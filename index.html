WHAT IS THE DEFINITION
THE SIMPLEST?
Blockchain is a public registry of who owns what
Chronologically and automatically updated
WHY THE BLOCKCHAIN ​​IS ALSO
A SOCIETAL INNOVATION?
It is a solution to social problems such as inequality, lack of trust and the need for transparency. It offers a new way to trust yourself, empower people and organize yourself differently.
This technology is based on a new way of trusting oneself when exchanging value on the internet.
WHY THE BLOCKCHAIN DO THE BUZZ?
1 It allows disintermediation
The exchange of information or to evaluate it is directly from person to person (or from computer to computer). Fewer intermediaries means less costs, more speed, performance and transparency.
2 It facilitates trust and transparency
Everyone shares the same information in real time and the exchanges are visible to the whole community. That is why it is distributed.
3 Participants operate this system
Instead of a single entity or central authority that holds the power. That is why it is decentralized.
4 It is ultra secure
Thanks to its architecture, it is extremely difficult to modify its content.
